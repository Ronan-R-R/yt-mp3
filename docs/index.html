ytmp3

Save YouTube audio on your own device. No ads, no junk, no server.

Windowsytmp3.exe, MP3 Linuxbinary, MP3 AndroidAPK, M4A

Three steps

1

Open the app

Download it for your device and open it. On Windows, if it warns about an unknown publisher, click More info then Run anyway.

2

Paste a link

Copy a YouTube video URL and paste it into the box. Pick where you want the file saved.

3

Hit download

The audio downloads and lands in your folder. That's it, the file is yours to keep.

Audio you have the rights to only. Respect creators.
