ytmp3

A tiny Windows app that saves YouTube audio as MP3. No ads, no junk, nothing to install.

Download for Windows
  1. Download and open ytmp3.exe. Windows may warn about an unknown publisher, click More info then Run anyway.
  2. Paste a YouTube link.
  3. Pick a folder and hit Download. Your MP3 lands there.
Audio you have the rights to only. Respect creators.
